Mission

  • Our primary mission is to fully legalize Cannabis for commercial use.
  • Our secondary mission is to fulfil Agenda 2030 of Sustainable Development Goal by 2030.
  • Our tertiary mission is to re-classify Cannabis from a gate-way drug to a cash crop.

Vision

Our Vision

Economic Transformation

An industry solely intended to boost Lesotho’s economy and address the needs of our people.

Legalization

An all-inclusive commercialised Cannabis industry fully sponsored by government for the benefit of our people.

Expansion

An industry guaranteed to unlock the financial potential of other plant-based medicines ultimately opening the window for clinical testing of traditional medicines for public use.
